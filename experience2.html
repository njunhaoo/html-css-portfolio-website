ABOUT EXPERIENCES PROJECTS

As part of NTU-PEAK, I had the opportunity to attend an overseas internship as a Supply Chain Intern at Oneject

My Role

As a team consisting of various majors, we were tasked with developing an innovating system aimed at addressing fluctuating demand challenges that were posed by the Pandemic

Accomplishments

• Utilized data analysis techniques to identify demand patterns based on different categories of customers, enabling data-driven decisions for dynamic pricing to be made

• Implemented demand-driven inventory replenishment process to decrease supply chain reaction time, putting less strain on production schedules

Difficulties Faced

The main difficulties faced were the lack of knowledge of the supply chain industry and the limited time available to us. As we were only given a month, it was difficult to create a concrete prototype to improve operational efficiency. Despite these challenges, I quickly adapted by conducting extensive research, collaborating with team members, and leveraging data analysis to propose feasible solutions.
